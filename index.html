About Us

We are a new football club founded in 2025 by 17-year-old Abdul Razzak, built to give poor youngsters the chance to chase their dreams through football. We are not yet officially registered, but we’re playing friendly matches and growing stronger every day.

Each player is required to bring Rs. 300 for every practice session to help cover field and basic training costs.
